Meditation #6

by Maceo

For this meditation, I was interested in remixing the chord progression demo from the notes. Chord progressions are something I have just recently started to get a better understanding of, and I thouroughly enjoyed playing around with the different scale and chord types.

When thinking of how to go about remixing this example, I took inspiration from my previous Meditation #3 to make it mood based (which I now also plan to implement for my final project). I wanted the chords to not only change sonically based on the selected mood but also reflect how different chord progressions can evoke distinct emotions. I copied over my mood functions and modified them to work with the synth rather than the player I had used before. After cleaning up the effects, I experimented with BPM settings and adjusted the chord progression variables and was left with three very different sounding chord progressions.
